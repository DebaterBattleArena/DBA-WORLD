ABOUT US.

DEBATER BATTLE ARENA (DBA) is an innovative platform showcasing high-level argumentative battles in the world of fiction. We've created a stage where the best debaters compete with their deep understanding of fictional universes, power systems, and your favorite characters. With in-depth analysis, sharp rhetoric, and critical thinking, every DBA debate is an adrenaline-fueled spectacle and a source of insight.

Our mission is to provide quality entertainment and education for the fictional community, fostering healthy discussion and finding out who truly understands the intricacies of fictional worlds beyond the limits of imagination.
